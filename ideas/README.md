# Idea archive

These dated notes are historical brainstorming records, not validated opportunities or active experiments. Scores and market claims in them were preliminary hypotheses and may be outdated.

An idea enters the public experiment ledger only after its customer, problem, offer, channel, metric, stop rule, and evidence plan are declared in an `experiment.json` record.

Use the GitHub nomination issue template for new proposals. Do not interpret this archive as financial advice, a recommendation, or evidence of demand.
